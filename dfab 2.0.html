    Hello Everyone! Be Safe and Wash Your Hands!

    EP1001 is a continuation of the previous Poly Wide Elective, EP1000. In this elective, Further Digital Fabrication and Prototyping, students will build on what they've learnt and applying the skills where possible. This elective also expands the boundaries of the previous elective by allowing the students to design and fabricate their own boards as well creating larger scale objects using the CNC machines or even molding and casting using resin. Students will be able to work on each aspects of this elective and work towards their final projects, incorporating everything that they have learnt.
