    Laptop Light Display:

    My desktop has always seemed very dull and lifeless. Then i wondered on how i would like to embelish it by adding lights and colors to it. Instead of looking for laptop light displays on the market, i thought of making my own one as its specifications would meet my liking and i can adjust anything where possible.

    Bill of Materials:

  • 2 Wires
  • 1 Attiny85
  • 1 Neopixel LED Strip
  • 1 Push Button Switch
  • 11 Pin Headers
  • 1 Resistor
  • 1 Capacitor
  • CNC Plywood 15mm
  • 1 Copper Board
